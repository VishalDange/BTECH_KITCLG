WELCOME TO KOLHAPUR INSTITUDE OF TECHNOLOGY KOLHAPUR

KIT's College of Engineering, Kolhapur is awarded “A” status by the Govt. of Maharashtra for delivering excellence in the field of academics and infrastructure. The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in the discipline of Biotechnology, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, CAD/ CAM/ CAE and many more. Spread over an area of 30 acres, and at a sufficient distance, though not very far off from the industrial hub, the institute is also known for its grand architectural blend of old and modern design and its environmental-friendly initiatives inside the campus.

The college has a total of 11 departments including Civil Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering etc. Spread across an area of 30 acre, KIT College of Engineering is also known for its spectacular architectural mixture of old and new architecture, as well as its on-campus environmental activities. The campus of the college has all the basic facilities for its students including a library, a cafeteria, an auditorium, a sports complex, a gym, well-equipped labs and separate hostel facilities for both boys and girls with all the necessary facilities.
